# App4 Create an app that sees if a person has a stomach virus or food poisoning by asking a series of four questions that will indicate the percentage they have each condition determined on symptoms. Stomach Diagnostic App

Stomach Virus or Food Poisoning?

Are you experiencing nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea? Answer a few short questions to find out if it's likely a stomach virus or food poisoning!

Get Started

The following code has been developed by students and/or researchers of the Freshman Research Initiative DIY Diagnostics Stream at The University of Texas at Austin. This code is shared for demonstration purposes and should not be considered a product. It is for entertainment purposes only.

THIS CODE SHOULD NOT BE USED TO DIAGNOSE ANY KIND OF MEDICAL CONDITION.

Question 1

Did your symptoms start within 6 hours of eating?

Question 2

Do you have a fever?

Question 3

Do you have body aches or chills?

Question 4

Have people around you recently had similar stomach symptoms?
